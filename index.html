Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

Overview

KibōFund is a personal budgeting app. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

Data We Collect

No personal data collected

KibōFund operates entirely on your device. All budget plans, spending records, goals, presets, and achievements are stored locally in your device's private app storage. No data is transmitted to our servers or any third party because we have no servers.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using your operating system's standard private file storage. This data is:

  • Accessible only to the KibōFund app
  • Not backed up to any cloud service operated by us
  • Removed when you uninstall the app

The optional backup/restore feature writes a .json file to a location you choose on your own device. We do not receive or access this file.

Third-Party Services

KibōFund does not integrate with any analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No third-party SDKs that collect user data are included in the app.

Permissions

Permission Purpose
Storage access (Android) Required only when you choose to export a backup file to a folder you select
File access (iOS) Required only when you choose to export or import a backup file

No other permissions are requested.

Children's Privacy

KibōFund does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Because we collect no data from anyone, the app is safe for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at the same URL with a revised date.
